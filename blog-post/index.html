Chess

What is chess?

Chess is a 2 player board game consisting of 64 squares with alternate colours (black and white). Each player has 16 pieces of which 8 pawns, 2 rooks, 2 bishops, 2 knights, a queen and a king. Each player takes alternate turns starting from white. The goal of the game is to checkmate the opponent king which simply means making him immobile while in check.

Why I like chess?

Chess is a game which involves a lot of planning and thinking. We have to execute our plan and expect the opponents plan. The ones who plans well, counters well and makes less number of mistakes wins.
